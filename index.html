Dr. Callum Furniss

Callum.Furniss@combined.nhs.uk

Project Areas:

Test anxiety & neuropsychological assessment

Past Literature:

Dorenkamp et al., 2020:

  • Developed questionnaire for test anxiety (Feelings About Neuropsychological Testing measure FANT) and validated this in non-clinical older adults (55+).

  • Correlated with other measures of anxiety and subjective cognitive concerns, showing some validity

Dorenkamp et al., 2023:

  • Test anxiety (on FANT) predicted (regression) performance on executive functioning measures, verbal learning and memory repetition errors, visual learning, and visual learning repetition errors, but not general anxiety (GAD-7)

Proposal:

  • Re-validation of the measure in a general clinical neuropsychology service for all ages (not just 55+).
  • Aim to replicate to replicate the finding that test anxiety, but not general anxiety is related to test performance.
  • Which cognitive areas does test anxiety impact most?

Why? helpful to in clinical practice to inform interpretation of NP results in cases why test anxiety is high
